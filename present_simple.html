Present Simple English Tense

 

Many people who taught me English told me that we simply have to remember these rules, but I decided to make it simpler.

And this is why I see English Grammar Tenses as math formulas, so to speak.

So to describe the Simple Present Tense we just have to use the next formula:

Subject + Verb (1st form) + _/s(es) + Object

Examples:

Subject Verb (1st form) + _/s(es) Object
I drink juice
You think twice
