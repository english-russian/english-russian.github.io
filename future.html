How to make Future from present tense

In my thoughts, I made a logical connection that to say something in the Future tense we just have to use Present Tense and just add helping verb "will" before the main verb :

Subject + will + Verb(1rd form) + _/s(es) + Object

Examples:

Subject will Verb(2rd form)/-ed Object
I will brake your vase.
You will drink my vodka.
She will walk tomorrow.
