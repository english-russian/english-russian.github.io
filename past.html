How to make Past from present tense

In my thoughts, I made a logical connection that to say something in the past we just have to use Present Tense and change the verb to the Second form (Past form): Subject + Verb(2rd form)/-ed -> Verb(2rd form)/-ed + Object Examples:

Subject Verb(2rd form)/-ed Object
I broke your vase.
You drank my vodka.
She walked yesterday.
